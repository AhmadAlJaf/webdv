Our Expectations

At Hot Bean's we expect applicants to have the appropriate qualification for the job they have applied for. So to make it clear, we have listed the academic Requirements for each job we provide.



Web Developer

Web developers need to have a post-graduate degree in computer science or equivalent.

Manager

Managers need to have a post-graduate degree in business administration or equivalent.

Production Worker

Production Workers need to have a minimum of 4 GSCES and past job experience in order to work for us.

Media Sales Consultant

For this job,applicants must have a undergraduate degree in Marketing or equivalent.
